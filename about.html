HOW WE BEGAN

CLUB HISTORY

2018

Our journey began in 2018, when a few fitness enthusiasts transformed a small garage into a makeshift gym for weekend boot camps and group yoga sessions. The energy and camaraderie quickly drew in friends and neighbors, laying the foundation for something bigger.

2019

By 2019, word had spread, and our member numbers started to grow. We officially formed our fitness club, rented our first dedicated space, and introduced structured classes. This year marked our commitment to building a true community—one focused on motivation, encouragement, and fun.

2020

In 2020, we upgraded our equipment, expanded our class offerings to include pilates and spin, and started hosting monthly community events. Despite the worldwide challenges that year, our club became a safe, supportive place for members to stay active and connected.

2022

With momentum building, 2022 was a transformative year. We welcomed new expert trainers, embraced technology for virtual workouts, and remodeled our club with state-of-the-art facilities. Our club reputation soared, and membership reached an all-time high.

2024

In 2024, we launched wellness workshops and participated in local charity runs, strengthening our bond with the broader community. Our mission grew beyond fitness—we became champions of holistic well-being for every member.

TODAY

Now, in 2025, our club has evolved into a thriving fitness family, united by our story and the shared belief that every member brings something new. Each year shaped who we are, and together, we continue inspiring, growing, and moving forward.
